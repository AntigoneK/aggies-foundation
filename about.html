About us

We remove cost barriers so children can stay in school — starting with tuition and essentials.

We work directly with schools and communities. Donations are routed through our foundation to the school, not to individuals. This keeps administration light and the impact real.

Our approach

We work directly with partner schools to keep children in class by covering tuition and essentials.

  • Direct-to-school funding
  • Clear, simple reporting
  • Local builders for classrooms

Classrooms in Kumi

Step by step, we help schools repair and build safe classrooms so learning can continue even when buildings are worn or overcrowded.
